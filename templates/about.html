User Guide

1) Enter the text that satisfies these constraints in the textbox:

 English language only will be accepted.
 The text must contain correct grammar and punctuations.
 The text preferred to be less than 10,000 characters with simple sentences.

2) Click The Submit button.


3) The system will move to the next page that contains the generated questions.

 The user can check the questions he needs by putting a mark in the checkbox beside the questions or he easily selects all.
 He can edit the questions or distractors in their text boxes.

4) After selecting all questions he needs to click the Download button to save his questions.


5) The system will save all questions that he selected in a text file on his Machine.
